About Us

This webpage has been built in coordination with the Department Of Computer Science and Engineering, JSSSTU, Mysuru, in concern with the final year project group formation of the students pursing the course Bachelor of Engineering in the department.

Guideline

The team leader can form a team of 4 members, where the process is followed as mentioned:

1. There are 3 groups: Group B, Group C and Group D. The team leader can choose to invite any number of students from each group. The invitations will be sent to all of them through Email.

2. The project team can have only one person from each group. So, the first student to accept the team leader's invitation in each group(i.e., one student each from Group B, Group C and Group D joins that team. )

3. Once the invitation is accepted by the student, that person cannot be removed from the team.

4. Later, after the group is formed that consists of a team leader and one person each from Group B, Group C and Group D, mentors will be allocated to each group.

NOTE: The team leaders will be given a certain time period to form the team. If within that period if the team leader fails to form the team then after the end of the specified period, random allocation of students to the group will be done. The students from each group can only join the team if they accept the invitation within the specified period.
